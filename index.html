Elias Azek the Majeye

Elias A Z E K is an artist, designer, and muralist living and working in Los Angeles.

Elias Azek draws from the abstract, images that often form themselves. As an artist and scribe, Elias Azek draws images of abstract surreal worlds often inhabited by entities and spirits conveying messages or reflections of the outer world. Azek brings forth beings from creation itself, from the world of thought into the physical.

A Z E K is the scribe. The Majeye is the message from the image. Every time presenting itself in a different form.
